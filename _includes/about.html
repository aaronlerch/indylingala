Oyo eza nini? What is this?

A group of people interested in the Lingala language and Congolese culture.

We're just getting started, but we plan on meeting once a month for general conversation and occasionally featuring presenters, music, movies, or anything else related to Lingala.

Yangana nani? Who’s invited?

You! Your friends! Anybody! We’re open to anyone who wants to come.

You might be a long time Lingala-speaker, or maybe hardly know about it at all but are interested. All are welcome to enjoy, learn, teach, and connect.

Eza wapi na tango nini? Where and when is it?

The club hasn't started yet, we are still getting the word out and gathering a list of interested folks. We'll meet in Indianapolis, likely on the northeast side of town, but that depends on who's in the club and where they live.

If you're interested, sign up for the newsletter to get information about our first meeting!
